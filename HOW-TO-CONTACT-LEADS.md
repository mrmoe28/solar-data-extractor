### üèõÔ∏è **County Permits** (Address ‚Üí Owner Lookup)

  - Score 90/100 - these are done deals
  - Contact them about: warranties, monitoring, battery add-ons

## üéØ Priority Contact Order

### 1. **Phone/Email in Post** (Score 70-100+)
- Call or email IMMEDIATELY
- These people WANT to be contacted
- They're ready to hire

### 2. **Craigslist Posts** (Check full ad)
- Click "Post URL" to see full listing
- Many include contact info in body even if not in title
- Often have phone buried in description

### 3. **Broken System Complaints** (Score 70-90)
- Social media posts about "not working" / "broken"
- DM them: "Saw your post. I specialize in solar troubleshooting. Can diagnose remotely or come out today."
- They're DESPERATE - original installer ghosting them

### 4. **Permit/Incentive Holders** (Do batch lookups)
- Export all addresses to CSV
- Use paid service for batch reverse lookup ($20-50 for 100 addresses)
- These convert extremely well - they already have solar

### 5. **General "Looking For" Posts**
- DM with: Quick intro + what you specialize in + offer free quote

- **County Assessor APIs** - Some counties have public APIs
- **Professional Skip Tracing Services** - $0.10-$0.50 per address
